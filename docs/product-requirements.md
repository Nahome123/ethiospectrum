# Product requirements

Ethiospectrum is a multilingual family-support platform for organizing information, understanding documents, tracking next steps, and finding educational resources in English, Amharic, and Spanish. This foundation delivers navigation, content structure, data design, and safe integration boundaries only. It does not provide authentication, uploads, AI analysis, messaging, scheduling, billing, or professional advice.

The product must distinguish verified document facts from general information, suggested next steps, professional questions, and unknowns. It must never promise benefits, services, eligibility, representation, legal advice, medical advice, immigration advice, or outcomes.
